🍰 Creamy Cheesecake Recipe 🍰

Try this creamy cheesecake in a crunchy graham cracker crust that’s beyond delicious. Getting the perfect no-crack bake is easier than you think, and you don’t need a water bath!

Ingredients

For the Crust:

  • 1½ cups graham cracker crumbs (180g)
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

For the Filling:

  • 3 blocks cream cheese, room temperature (8-ounce each/227g)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (200g)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract (10mL)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 3 large eggs, room temperature
  • ½ cup sour cream, room temperature (120g)

Instructions

For the Crust:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Stir until it feels like wet sand.
  3. Press mixture into a 9-inch springform pan.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes. Let it cool.

For the Filling:

  1. Beat cream cheese in a large mixing bowl until creamy.
  2. Add sugar, salt, vanilla, and lemon juice. Mix until smooth.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing after each addition.
  4. Mix in sour cream until well combined.
  5. Pour filling into crust. Bake at 300°F for 30-35 minutes, then cool and refrigerate for 6 hours.

Nutrition

Per Serving:

  • Calories: 388 kcal
  • Carbs: 30 g
  • Protein: 6 g
  • Fat: 27 g
  • Sugar: 24 g
