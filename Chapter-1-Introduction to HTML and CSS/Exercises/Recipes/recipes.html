Strawberry Cheesecake

Try this creamy cheesecake in a crunchy graham cracker crust that’s beyond delicious. Getting the perfect no-crack bake is easier than you think, and you don’t need a water bath!

Ingredients

    For the Crust:

  • 1½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

    • For the Filling:

  • 3 (8-ounce) blocks cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 3 large eggs, room temperature
  • ½ cup sour cream, room temperature

Instructions

    For the Crust:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Combine graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar in a bowl and press into a springform pan.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes and let cool.

    4. For the Filling:

  4. Beat cream cheese, sugar, salt, vanilla, and lemon juice until smooth.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well. Finally, mix in sour cream.
  6. Pour the filling into the crust and bake for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 300°F and bake for another 30–35 minutes.
  7. Turn off the oven and leave cheesecake inside for 1 hour. Cool at room temperature, then chill for at least 6 hours before serving.
