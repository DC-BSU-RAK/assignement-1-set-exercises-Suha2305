Overview

Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to explore, build, and survive in a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world. You can gather resources, craft tools, and create your own adventures.

Key Information

Developer: Mojang Studios

Release Date: November 18, 2011

Platforms: PC, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, Mobile

Cast & Creative Team

Director: Jared Hess

Main Cast: Jack Black (Steve), Jason Momoa, Danielle Brooks

Characters

Steve, Alex, Creepers, Enderman, and more!
